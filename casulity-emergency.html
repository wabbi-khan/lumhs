Causality and Emergency Services

Our causality and emergency department provides immediate medical attention to critical and life-threatening emergencies as well as treatment for urgent medical problems to patients 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Our medical specialists are well-trained to handle emergencies.

Our multidisciplinary team of specialists provides quality and personalized care for conditions such as (but not limited to):

Abdominal pain

Cold and flu

Cuts and bruises

Fever

Headaches

Dengue fever

Mild burns and scalds

Nausea and vomiting

Nosebleed

Sore eyes

Strains and sprains

Urinary tract infection (UTI)

Asthma

Acute gout

Insect bites and stings

Appendicitis

Gallstones

Kidney stones




Liaquat University Hospital (LUH) in Sindh is a multispeciality hospital providing complete medical as well as surgical care.
