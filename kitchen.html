Kitchen Services

Food is fundamental to the human experience, and that’s doubly true for the patient experience, since nutrition is key to patient health.Our team has the necessary experience and qualifications to work in this environment.

Liaquat University Hospital provides a great service to the patients with a good deal of variety and quality diet in the food offerings. The main kitchen in a hospital prepares a tremendous amount of food for distribution among the patients. Holding equipment/Trollies is imperative so the food maintains proper temperatures for the patient's sake as well as the employees and visitors.

Patient`s nutrition, care and recovery are very important to us. Our physician and dietitian evaluate their medical status and prescribe a diet appropriate for their medical condition.

Daily 2000 patient’s meal is being served in Liaquat University Hospital Hyderbad.
