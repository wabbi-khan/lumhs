Out-Patient Services

The Liaquat University Hospital, provides general out-patient services and is designed for the maximum comfort and privacy of patients in the course of consultation with advanced equipment and highly trained staff. Staff members at out-patient department are well trained and experienced in the service, we provide.
Number of Post-Graduates has also been increased to accommodate patient need. All of the care that you need before, during, and after the procedure, surgery, or test may be conveniently provided in one place.

Visiting Hours:

Out-patient services are available between 8am – 8pm.
